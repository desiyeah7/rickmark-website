Cybersecurity Notes

Introduction

Cybersecurity refers to the practice and
technologies used to protect computers, networks,
and data from attacks ,damage ,or unauthorized
access.
Topics Covered:

1.Types of Cyber Security Threats

  • Malware
  • Phishing
  • Ransomware
  • Keylogging
  • Social enginering
  • Insider threaats
2.Security Models

Bell-Lapadula Model:

Focuses on maintaining confidentiality by
preventing unauthorized reading of data.

Biba Model:

Focuses on integrity-preventing unauthorized
writing of data.

Chinese Wall Model:

Prevent conflict of interest by resticting access to
certain information based on previous access.
3.Information &Computer Safety Practices
  • Use strong passwords
  • Enable two-factor authorization
  • Update software regularly
  • Avoid suspicious links
  • Backup data
  • Usr antivirus software
Summary: Cybersecurity is crucial for protecting digital
information. Understanding threats and applying
safety practices helps prevent attacks.
